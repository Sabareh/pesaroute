# PesaRoute Roadmap

## Phase 0: Foundation

- Monorepo structure for API, web, mobile, and docs.
- Django models, serializers, routes, migrations, and seed command.
- Deterministic scam checker.
- Educational simulators for MMFs, Treasury bills, SACCOs, and global routes.
- Next.js landing page and passport placeholders.
- Expo consumer app skeleton with mock data.

## Phase 1: Consumer MVP

- Auth flow and onboarding.
- API-backed mobile screens.
- Better simulator UX and saved simulation history.
- Journal review reminders.
- Portfolio mirror editing, summary, and privacy controls.
- Catalog search and filters.

## Phase 2: Trust and Compliance

- Rate limiting and abuse controls.
- Stronger audit views for sensitive actions.
- Admin workflows for product passport version review.
- Professional verification workflow.
- Consent grant UX with expiry and revocation.

## Phase 3: Professional Review

- Consultation request triage.
- Time-limited data sharing.
- Professional notes and user-visible review summaries.
- No booking or payment integration until policy, compliance, and consent flows are ready.

## Phase 4: Scale

- Caching for public catalog endpoints.
- Background jobs for imports and scheduled checks.
- Observability, metrics, and error tracking.
- Security review and penetration testing.
