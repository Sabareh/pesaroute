PHASE 2.7 PROMPT 1 — Build Kenya Investment Knowledge Base data architecture

We have already implemented the PesaRoute learning engine prompts. The app now needs a real Kenyan investment knowledge base and data foundation.

Product context:
PesaRoute is a Kenya-first investment decision platform. It helps users learn, simulate, compare investment routes, keep a private investment journal, manually mirror their portfolio, and request review from verified professionals.

PesaRoute does NOT:

* hold user investment money
* execute investments
* act as a broker
* act as a fund manager
* ask for M-Pesa PIN
* ask for bank passwords
* ask for broker credentials
* promise returns
* give unlicensed investment advice

Goal:
Create a structured Kenya Investment Knowledge Base that powers:

* learning tracks
* product passports
* route engine
* simulators
* scam checker
* provider/professional marketplace
* future scheduled data pipelines

Important:
Do not implement aggressive web scraping yet.
Do not add AI.
Do not add M-Pesa.
Do not add payments.
Do not add investment execution.
Do not add account linking.
Do not scrape NSE market prices unless terms/permissions are explicitly handled.
Build a safe, auditable data architecture first.

Public/official source categories to support:

1. CMA

   * licensees
   * fund managers
   * approved collective investment schemes
   * investment advisers
   * stockbrokers
   * REIT managers
   * other capital market intermediaries

2. CBK

   * Treasury bills
   * Treasury bonds
   * DhowCSD education/reference pages
   * auction results
   * auction schedules

3. NSE

   * listed companies
   * listed securities categories
   * official market data service references
   * do not redistribute restricted market data without permission

4. SASRA

   * licensed deposit-taking SACCOs
   * authorised non-withdrawable deposit-taking SACCOs
   * restricted/no-longer-authorised SACCO status where available

5. RBA

   * registered fund managers
   * registered custodians
   * retirement benefits service providers
   * pension education content references

6. IRA

   * licensed insurers
   * licensed insurance brokers
   * licensed intermediaries
   * retirement/insurance-adjacent product education references

7. Manual curated sources

   * provider websites
   * public PDFs
   * official circulars
   * educational pages
   * internal editorial notes
