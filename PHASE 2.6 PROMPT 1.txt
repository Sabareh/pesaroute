PHASE 2.6 PROMPT 1 — Build the PesaRoute Learning Engine

We are adding a DataCamp-inspired learning experience to PesaRoute, but adapted for investment literacy.

Important:
Do not copy DataCamp branding, assets, icons, UI, or exact product structure. Borrow only the learning patterns:

* learning tracks
* courses
* lessons
* practice
* flashcards
* XP
* streaks
* progress
* resources
* premium unlocks

Product context:
PesaRoute is a Kenya-first investment decision platform. It helps users learn, simulate, compare investment routes, keep a private investment journal, manually mirror their portfolio, and request review from verified professionals.

PesaRoute does NOT:

* hold user investment money
* execute investments
* act as a broker
* act as a fund manager
* ask for M-Pesa PIN
* ask for bank passwords
* ask for broker credentials
* promise returns
* give unlicensed investment advice

Goal:
Create a learning engine where users can move through investment literacy tracks and connect learning to simulators, journals, scam checks, and professional review.

Learning loop:
Learn → Practice → Simulate → Journal → Review
