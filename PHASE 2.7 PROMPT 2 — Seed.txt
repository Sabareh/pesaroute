* abbreviation: CMA
* scope: capital markets, fund managers, CIS/unit trusts, investment advisers, stockbrokers, REITs, online forex/derivatives categories where relevant

Central Bank of Kenya:

* abbreviation: CBK

Nairobi Securities Exchange:

* abbreviation: NSE
* scope: listed companies, securities exchange, official market data services

Insurance Regulatory Authority:

* abbreviation: IRA
* scope: insurance and insurance-linked providers

Kenya Revenue Authority:

* abbreviation: KRA
* scope: tax education references only, not tax advice

3. Seed product categories:

Money Market Funds:

* beginner summary
* risk: low to medium
* liquidity: high
* typical use: emergency fund, short-term savings, cash parking
* beginner mistakes:

  * chasing highest yield only
  * ignoring fees
  * ignoring withdrawal timelines
  * assuming returns are guaranteed
  * not checking fund manager/regulatory status
* simulator link: MMF simulator

Treasury Bills:

* risk: low
* liquidity: medium
* typical use: short-term government securities
* notes:

  * explain 91, 182, 364-day tenors
  * explain DhowCSD
  * explain auction concept
  * explain maturity
* simulator link: T-bill simulator

Treasury Bonds:

* risk: low to medium
* liquidity: lower than T-bills for beginners
* notes:

  * coupon
  * yield
  * maturity
  * secondary market concept
  * interest rate risk

SACCOs:

* risk: medium
* liquidity: medium to low depending on rules
* notes:

  * deposits vs share capital
  * dividends/rebates
  * loan multipliers
  * guarantor risk
  * SASRA status

Chamas:

* risk: varies
* liquidity: varies
* notes:

  * governance
  * minutes
  * contribution discipline
  * land investment risk
  * group decision policy

NSE Stocks:

* risk: medium to high
* liquidity: market-dependent
* notes:

  * dividends
  * capital gains/losses
  * CDS account
  * stockbroker
  * diversification
  * do not treat as short-term emergency money

US Stocks and ETFs:

* risk: medium to high
* notes:

  * currency risk
  * platform risk
  * ETF vs individual stock
  * dividends/withholding tax basics
  * long-term horizon

REITs:

* risk: medium
* notes:

  * listed/property exposure
  * income distribution concept
  * liquidity and market risk

Land:

* risk: medium to high
* liquidity: low
* notes:

  * due diligence
  * title search
  * lawyer/surveyor
  * pressure sales
  * deposit caution

Bitcoin and Crypto Risk:

* risk: high
* notes:

  * volatility
  * self-custody
  * scams
  * regulatory uncertainty
  * not equivalent to MMF or Treasury bill

Fixed Deposits:

* risk: low to medium
* liquidity: low/medium
* notes:

  * bank lock-in
  * early withdrawal
  * interest rate
  * compare with MMF/T-bills

Pension Products:

* risk: depends
* liquidity: long-term
* notes:

  * retirement planning
  * RBA regulated context
  * long horizon

8. Seed scam red flags:

* guaranteed high returns
* monthly returns above normal market claims
* recruitment/referral requirement
* pressure to send money quickly
* no clear regulator
* no named custodian/trustee
* unclear withdrawal terms
* refusal to share documents
* fake screenshots/testimonials
* land doubling claims
* forex/crypto bot cannot lose
* celebrity/influencer-only proof
* WhatsApp-only investment scheme
